MSc Degree Qualifications

Applicants should have a Bachelor''s degree in a relevant area from an accredited institution with a minimum grade of "Good". Applicant may be accepted if (s)he holds a post-Bachelor''s Diploma in a relevant area with a minimum GPA of 3.00/4.00 (or Very Good).

Doctoral Degree Qualifications

Applicants should have MSc degree in the field of specification, either from the academy or from any accredited university. The Graduate Studies at each College and Institute in the Academy identifies the applicants who are eligible for enrollment into the program. The academic department then interviews the applicants individually to those who will be recommended for admission. Applications of newly admitted applicants are valid for two semesters. If an admitted applicant does not enroll by the second semester, (s)he must reapply for admission.
